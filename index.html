The Problem

Emergency triage is broken
by pressure and inconsistency.

U.S. emergency departments handle 145 million visits annually. Triage errors affect an estimated 1 in 3 ED encounters, contributing to preventable deaths and over $28,000 in avoidable cost per adverse event.

1
🏥

Overcrowding

Emergency departments routinely operate beyond capacity, forcing nurses to triage faster with less information.

2
⚡

High-Pressure Decisions

Triage nurses make critical urgency calls in under 5 minutes with incomplete patient history and no second opinion.

3
⚖️

Inconsistency

Studies show the same patient can receive different ESI levels depending on who performs the triage — variability that costs lives.

4
📉

Resource Waste

Over-triage of low-acuity patients consumes beds, staff time, and equipment that critical patients need immediately.

Our Solution

MedIQ turns intake data
into clinical intelligence.

A two-layer AI system: XGBoost predicts triage urgency, GPT-4 generates the clinical rationale. The nurse stays in control — MedIQ makes sure no critical patient is invisible at intake.

🎯

ESI Urgency Prediction

XGBoost classifier predicts ESI level 1–5 from vitals, demographics, and chief complaints at intake.

📋

GPT-4 Clinical Rationale

Generative AI consumes the prediction and produces a plain-English summary, next steps, and warning flags.

👥

Live Patient Queue

Patients are auto-ranked by urgency so the doctor always sees the most critical case first.

🔒

Human-in-the-Loop by Design

Every output is flagged for clinician review. MedIQ supports judgment — it never replaces it.

Model Performance

Optimized for the metric
that matters most.

Overall accuracy is a misleading metric for imbalanced medical datasets. We optimize for ESI-1 recall — correctly flagging critical patients — because a false negative risks patient death.

Responsible AI

Built with ethics
at every layer.

MedIQ is a decision-support tool, not a decision-making system. Every design choice reflects that distinction.

⚖️

Bias & Fairness

Full fairness audit across race, ethnicity, gender, and language. Lowest-performing groups are flagged as priority retraining targets. Error rates monitored continuously.

🔒

Privacy & HIPAA

No patient data is stored beyond the active session. All inputs are processed in-memory. Production deployment would require full HIPAA-compliant infrastructure.

👩‍⚕️

Human Oversight

Every MedIQ output requires clinician review before a triage decision is recorded. The system cannot admit, discharge, diagnose, or prescribe — by design.

⚠️

Failure Modes

False negatives risk delayed treatment and patient death. Liability falls on the clinician and hospital — not the AI. This drives our recall-first optimization strategy.

🛡️

Hallucination Guardrails

GPT-4 runs at temperature 0.3 to minimize variability. Prompt explicitly prohibits diagnosis. All outputs carry mandatory clinician review disclaimer.

📊

Transparency

Every prediction includes full ESI probability breakdown so clinicians can see model confidence — not just the top prediction. No black box outputs.
